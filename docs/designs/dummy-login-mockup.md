## Recommended Approach

**Option 3 (with divider)** is recommended because:

1. **Clear separation**: The "or" makes it obvious these are alternative paths
2. **Good visibility**: Demo option won't be missed
3. **Familiar pattern**: Users understand "or" dividers from other login pages
4. **Mobile-friendly**: Stacks vertically well on small screens
5. **Not overwhelming**: Doesn't dominate the page like Option 4
